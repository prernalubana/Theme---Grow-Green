Plants are nature’s gift as they are beneficial for us in many ways.
Plants play a significant role in our lives. Without plants, there would be no more life existing on planet earth. There are around three to four lakh identified plant species, and this list is still increasing. They provide us with a variety of things to fulfil our daily requirements, including food to eat, air to breathe, clothes to cover our body, wood, medicine, shelter, and many products for human benefit.
